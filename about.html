About Hi, I'm Himara

In My Own Words

As a Biotechnology undergraduate, I see science not just as a subject, but as a way to inspire innovation and discovery. I am fascinated by how research and technology can work together to improve lives. Every challenge is an opportunity to grow, and I aim to build my future by turning ideas into solutions that matter.

How I Got Here

My journey began with a strong foundation in science during my school years at Holy Family Girls' School, Wennappuwa, where I completed the G.C.E. Advanced Level examination in the Biology stream. This experience deepened my curiosity about living systems and strengthened my passion for exploring the molecular and cellular mechanisms of life. Driven by this interest, I pursued higher studies in Biotechnology at SLIIT, where I continue to expand my knowledge and skills. Each step has shaped me into a learner eager to connect scientific understanding with real-world applications, preparing me for the path ahead in research and innovation.

My Values & Beliefs

Curiosity

I believe curiosity is the driving force behind all discoveries. Asking questions and seeking answers helps me grow as a learner and explore the unknown in science.

Integrity

I value honesty and responsibility in learning and research. I strive to approach every challenge with sincerity and respect for knowledge.

Collaboration

I believe great ideas are built together. Working with others not only improves results but also builds strong, meaningful connections in science and beyond.

Innovation

I value creativity and forward-thinking, and I aim to contribute ideas that push boundaries and bring positive change to society through biotechnology.

Why Work With Me
